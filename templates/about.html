About Resume Analyzer

AI-Powered Job Application Helper

Resume Analyzer is an advanced tool that helps job seekers optimize their applications using artificial intelligence.

How It Works

1. Upload Your Resume

Upload your resume in PDF format. Our system extracts the content and analyzes it to understand your skills and experience.

2. Job Compatibility Analysis

Enter a job description and company details. We'll analyze how well your resume matches the job requirements and identify strengths and gaps.

3. Resume Optimization

Get a tailored version of your resume optimized for the specific job, highlighting relevant skills and experiences.

4. Skill Enhancement Plan

Receive a personalized plan to develop the skills you need to become a stronger candidate, including recommended resources and courses.

Powered by AI

Resume Analyzer uses advanced AI models to provide accurate and helpful analysis. The platform leverages Google's Gemini AI to understand both your resume and job requirements, offering insights that would typically require a career counselor or HR professional.

Features

  • Resume text extraction
  • Initial resume analysis
  • Job compatibility scoring
  • Skills gap identification
  • Resume optimization
  • Personalized skill development plan
  • Downloadable reports

Data Privacy

Your resume and job details are processed securely. We do not store your personal information for longer than necessary to provide our services.

All analysis is performed using secure AI processing that respects your privacy.
